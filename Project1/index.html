OUR RECRUITMENT PROCESS

01 Application Your journey starts here! Submit your updated CV (Resume) and a compelling Cover Letter via our online application portal. We are looking beyond mere qualifications; this is your chance to showcase your passion, unique perspective, and why your career goals align perfectly with DataFlow's mission to innovate.
02 Technical Tests Time to shine with your skills. Successful applicants will be invited to complete a practical technical challenge (often a concise coding assignment or a problem-solving scenario). This test is designed to objectively assess your core competencies and your ability to apply clear, scalable logic to real-world tech problems.
03 Interview This is a two-way conversation. You'll meet with our Hiring Manager and Team Lead for a mix of in-depth technical discussions and behavioral questions. We explore your past projects, teamwork style, and how you approach challenges. It's also your opportunity to ask questions and discover more about our team culture and vision.
04 Offer Letter Congratulations! Upon successful completion of all stages, the DataFlow Recruitment Team will extend an official Offer Letter. This formal document will outline your position, compensation package, benefits, and start date. We look forward to welcoming you aboard and beginning the next chapter of innovation together!
